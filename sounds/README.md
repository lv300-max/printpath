# PrintPath Micro Sounds

All sounds should be:
- Under 0.5 seconds
- Low volume (mixed at -18dB or lower)
- No looping
- WAV or MP3, mono

## Files needed
- tick.mp3     — soft mechanical click (button tap)
- whoosh.mp3   — quick air sweep (design generated)
- ding.mp3     — clean high note (print ready)
- snap.mp3     — soft lock/snap (save project)
- thud.mp3     — soft low thud (continue/add to cart)
- shuffle.mp3  — quick swipe/ruffle (regenerate)
